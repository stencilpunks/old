Morning Glory Stencils
*

The Morning Glory logo is called "The Crown & The Down". Here is the band's description of it: "The crown is humanity's wealthy & powerful 3% who control the entire globe & populace from markets & media to perception & thought. The down are those who make the growing masses of poor & disenfranchized.Cast with the lot of being the foundatationof thier oppressors. The two are seperated by barbed wire. This is the symbol of humanity's division & nature."
